Driving License Application Process

A driving license is an important government-issued document that allows you to drive a vehicle on public roads. In India, driving licenses are issued by the Regional Transport Office (RTO) through various RTO offices and online portals.

Apply for a Driving License

To apply for a driving license in India, follow these steps:

Visit the official Parivahan Sewa website to begin your application.

Click on the "Apply Online" option and select your state.

Fill in the necessary personal and contact information and upload the required documents, such as proof of identity, address proof, and photograph.

Book a slot for your driving test and visit the RTO office on the scheduled date and time.

Take the driving test and wait for your driving license to be issued by the RTO.

Please note that the processing time for a driving license can vary depending on the completeness and accuracy of your application, as well as other factors.
